A language model that runs in your browser

Pick one of three small open-weights models (Qwen 2.5, Llama 3.2, or Gemma 2). They are nowhere near as capable as the frontier LLMs you are used to. The model downloads to your browser, runs on your own device via WebGPU, and stays cached for future visits. No backend, no API key, and your messages and the model's responses never leave your device.
